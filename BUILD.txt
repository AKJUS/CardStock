PyInstaller commands for building the standalone Designer and Viewer apps on Mac and Windows:

Mac:
    pyinstaller --onedir --clean -y --windowed -s -n standalone standalone.py
    mkdir dist/containerDir
    mv dist/standalone.app dist/containerDir/
    pyinstaller --onedir --clean -y --windowed -s --exclude-module PyInstaller --add-data dist/containerDir:. -n CardStock_Designer designer.py
    pyinstaller --onedir --clean -y --windowed -s -n CardStock_Viewer viewer.py

Windows:
    pyinstaller --onefile --clean -y --windowed -n standalone standalone.py
    pyinstaller --onefile --clean -y --windowed --exclude-module PyInstaller --add-data dist/standalone.exe:. -n CardStock_Designer designer.py
    pyinstaller --onefile --clean -y --windowed -n CardStock_Viewer viewer.py
